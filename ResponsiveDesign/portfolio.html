Final FIght

Link aproaches Demise where he tells Link what has happened to him and giving Link one last chance to turn away. Link denies and so the 2 start to battle, after a long fight is finally able to weaken the demon using the Master Sword. Link takes the final blow onto Demise, but in his last breaths he curses himself, Link, and Zelda into a never ending carnation recreating the 3 into battle over and over until he is able to win. Link is finally able to save Zelda and go back into present time , at this time Zelda is able to finally lower Skyloft back onto the ground since the evil of Demise is no longer a threat. Link and Zelda decide to live a life together and create a new society on the land.
