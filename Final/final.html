The Final Fight

When Zelda releases Ganon, it almost looks like he hacthed from a cacoon, destroying the floor both him and Link fall to a hidden chamber inside the castle where they fight. The champions pioliting the divine beasts, aim their sights across the land at the castle slightly weakening Ganon to help Link in his fight. After a long battle Calamity Ganon transforms into his beast form escaping the castle onto Hyrule field. Link of course follows him, where Zelda gives Link the light arrows to defeat the beast. Eventually Link shoots a light arrow at Ganons head destroying all the evil of the calamity.
