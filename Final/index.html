About the Game

This game starts 100 years after Link was put into a deep healing slumber. An ancient prophecy of a calamity had finally came true, causing destruction almost everywhere. In a attempt of stopping the evil Link and Zelda fight the enemies which Link get sevrely injured, making Zelda put him into a healing chamber. Zelda then sacrifices her life into holding the evil calamity in Hyrule Castle where it cannot escape.

The Beginning of the Adventure

When Link awakens from his sleep and exits the chamber, he is greeted to the voice of Zelda telling him to destroy calamity Ganon, but Link has lost his memory of the past events. Link then finds a old man sitting next to a fire, after a short conversation Link is tasked by the old man to awaken what is called shiekah towers and shrines and then to complete the challenges within the shrines. After doing this and exploring the great platue, where Link is currently at, the old man confronts link has the dead spirit of the king of hyrule explaing to link what happened and what Link must do.
