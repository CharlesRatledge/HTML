The Legend of Zelda Ocarina of Time

About the Game

The Legend of Zelda Ocarina of Time is a game where you explore a world and complete puzzles and tasks throughout. The main objectives of this game come from a series of level based structures called dungeons. Within these dungeons there are many puzzles and enemys you must overcome to progress through. Outside of these dungeons there are many required and side quests, the required quests often either open a way forward or give you an item required to progress through to beat the game. The side quests however are not optinal, but will give you helpful power ups like extra health or a stronger sword for example.

The game starts a young boy named Link waking up and being told by an old talking tree about his destiny. There is an evil demon named Ganon who is attempting to take the 3 pieces of the triforce, an ancient object that gives the user a wish, to take over the world. The 3 pieces are hidden within 3 seperate people being Ganon, Link, and Zelda who is the princess of the kingdom. Link is first tasked to collect 3 jewls to open an old temple with a weapon called the master sword. This sword has the power to kill the demon, but when Link pulls it out of the pedalstall he is put to sleep to wake up 7 years in the future. Now Ganon has cursed much of the kingdom and zelda went into hiding. Link is then tasked to save 7 important people called sages, who have the power to help link kill Ganon. After saving all the sages Link and Zelda meetup finally giving Ganon the chance to capture Zelda and lure link into a battle. In attempt to save Zelda, the sages help Link into Ganon's castle and the fight between the 2 happens. In the end Link kills Ganon which clears away all of his curses and the kingdom and Zelda are saved.
